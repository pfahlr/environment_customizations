[2025-07-03 09:55:55 PM] To put cursors at the beginning of each line in VSCode. For example, to insert comment markers in a language that doesn't support multiline comments like yaml:
1. Select the lines you wish to edit
2. Press `Alt+Shift+i` 
3. Press `home` (might take a few presses in some cases).
4. The cursor will be at the start of each line and you can add your `#` or whatever. 

In summary:
`Alt+Shift+i` puts cursors on each selected line
